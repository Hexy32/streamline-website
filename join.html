HOW TO JOIN

1.

The first thing todo on your mission to join Streamline is easy! Just join the Discord server. After that you will be able to chat with current Streamliners and you will have access to the application!

At this point if you have any questions you can DM any staff members.

2.

The next thing is also quite simple, just fill out this Google Form, this form is how we make sure that the best of people get into Streamline, with an acceptence rate of around 85%. We belive that you will make it!

You can also find the form in the #applications-and-info channel on the Discord.

3.

Once you are done filling out the form, just wait until you get alerted that you have been accepted, or you will get a DM if your application gets declined, you can contact staff if you want to redo the application.
